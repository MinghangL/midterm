About me

My name is Minghang(Morton) Liang, My interest in web design stems from my fascination with visual communication and user experience. I enjoy exploring how design can enhance user interaction on websites, and I‘ve also been learning front-end development languages like HTML, CSS to bring these designs to life. Throughout my studies, I have gained a solid understanding of fundamental web design principles and how to use elements like color, typography, and layout to create aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly websites.

Looking ahead, I plan to delve deeper into responsive design and user experience (UX) design, and I hope to work on real-world web design projects in the future, where I can apply my skills and knowledge.
